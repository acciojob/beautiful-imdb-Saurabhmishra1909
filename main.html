The Dark Knight

2008 • PG-13 • 2h 32m

When the menace known as the Joker wreaks havoc and chaos on the people of Gotham, Batman must accept one of the greatest psychological and physical tests of his ability to fight injustice. Christian Bale delivers a powerful performance as Batman, while Heath Ledger's portrayal of the Joker earned him a posthumous Academy Award for Best Supporting Actor. Christopher Nolan's masterpiece redefined the superhero genre with its dark tone and complex characters.
